About the fund

The way to the sports development and support

The main aspects of the sports support and development program is establishment and maintenance of successful functioning of a set of measures aimed at health promotion, formation of spiritual perfection and healthy lifestyle both individual personality and the whole nation.

Program goals include:

  • implementation of physical culture and sports in the everyday life of the population in order to achieve and maintain physical and moral perfection, that has a direct influence on the health of the nation;
  • creation of the most suitable conditions in the sector of sport and recreation for optimal accordance with the needs of the population with the aim of professional sports promotion;
  • development and popularization of children and youth sports;
  • support and development of the Olympic sports movement principles;
  • provision of full support to sports veterans, various sports organizations, as well as to the movement of disabled athletes;.

Program objectives are:

  • creation of favorable conditions providing the availability of sports activities and physical education for the population;
  • education of physical and sports culture for children, youth and adults;
  • development of the base and the necessary conditions in order to attract individuals with disabilities and invalids to engage in sports;
  • creation of modern and universal material and technical base that meets the high international requirements of the Olympic Committee;
  • organization of fruitful and effective international cooperation in this field;
  • provision of comfortable conditions for the development of the sports industry by attracting outside investments;

List of subprograms:

  1. Support for the development of professional and mass sports.
  2. Development and constant improvement of infrastructure, material and technical base for sports activities.
  3. Provision of complex rehabilitation and recreational activities for athletes and sports veterans.
  4. Provision of housing conditions for athletes, sports veterans and disabled athletes.

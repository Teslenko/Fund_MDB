Our sports teams

Burevisnyk sports team

Burevisnyk is Kyiv professional Dragon boat rowing club which is based at Hydropark in Kyiv. Dragon boat is one of the fastest growing sports in Ukraine. It has the international status of competition and definitely wants to glorify Ukraine at the international level. The team exists since 2007 and has no material support since its establishment till present. The team is trained solely by the enthusiasm, the members of the team take part in competitions at their own expense, incur other expenses associated with this kind of sports.


Wrestling school children and youth sports school

Wrestling school is Kyiv Greco-Roman wrestling club. It is one of the promising Kyiv clubs. The athletes of the club are the winners of international and all-Ukrainian Greco-Roman wrestling tournaments.


Spartak sports team

Spartak is Mykolaiv Greco-Roman wrestling club. The team exists since 2008 till present. Children from 7 years and higher attend Spartak club. The athletes of the club are the prizewinners of Greco-Roman wrestling Championships of Ukraine and international Greco-Roman wrestling tournaments.
